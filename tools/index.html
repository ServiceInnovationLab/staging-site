Process

As a design and development lab working across government agencies the lab developed a process for its work to follow. Similar to other innotvation process it creates space and time to better undertstand the issues before moving to ideation, development and testing.

For each of the phases we have provided a summary of its key features, reflections and tips from the lab team and links to a range of the tools and templates.
